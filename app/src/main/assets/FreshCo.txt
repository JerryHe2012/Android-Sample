FreshCo Ltd. is a Canadian discount supermarket chain owned by Sobeys Inc. It was launched in May 2010. As of March 2015, there were 88 FreshCo stores, all located in Ontario. A similarly named supermarket in Chennai, India, is unrelated.
In December 2017, Sobeys Inc. announced plans to re-brand up to 64 stores in western Canada currently under the Safeway and Sobeys names into the FreshCo banner.
FreshCo's main competitors in Canada include Nofrills (Loblaws) and Food Basics (Metro).
