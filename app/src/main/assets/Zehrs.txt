Zehrmart Ltd. (doing business as Zehrs Markets or simply Zehrs), is a Canadian supermarket chain in southern Ontario. The chain has 43 locations and is a part of Loblaw Companies Limited. The first store was established by Emory Zehr and his sons in Kitchener in 1950. Until 1995, the store's logo was a red or orange square, with two small lines to make it a boldened stylized "Z".
